== Unordered Lists

Blank lines are required before and after a list. To create an unordered list, type an asterisk, a space, and then some text.

----

* Jeff Beard

* Steve Flatt

* Jordan Robinson-Rucker

* Andy Smith

* Derek Adams

----

* Jeff Beard

* Steve Flatt

* Jordan Robinson-Rucker

* Andy Smith

* Derek Adams

To nest list items, use additional asterisks.

----

* Clay Tison

** Jeff Beard

** Steve Flatt

** Jordan Robinson-Rucker

*** Josh Rucker

** Andy Smith

** Derek Adams

* Helen Follett

----

* Clay Tison

** Jeff Beard

** Steve Flatt

** Jordan Robinson-Rucker

*** Josh Rucker

** Andy Smith

* Helen Follett
